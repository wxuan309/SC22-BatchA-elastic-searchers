The Why

According to the CDC, diabetes is a chronic disorder that impacts how a personâ€™s body converts food into energy. Diabetes slows down or stops the production of insulin, increasing the amount of glucose, or blood sugar. This increase leads to heart disease, vision loss, and other serious health-problems. Our objective is to spread awareness on the topic of diabetes. It is something that a person can carry through their life without knowing. The purpose of our website is to aid people in self-evaluation to see whether they may or may not be more prone to having diabetes.

Exploration Of Our Data

Our data is derived from a 2019 Diabetes data set from Kaggle. The evaluated factors include: Age, Gender, Family Diabetes, High Blood Pressure, Prediabetes, Physical Active, BMI, Smoking, Alcohol, Sleep, Sound Sleep, Regular Medicine, Junk Food, Stress, BP level, Pregnancies, Urination, and Diabetic.

  • June 2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were told to create a project on something that was of our interest. Since diabetes is an on going complication, we figured that we would create a website to help people self-diagnose themselves to see if they could be likely to have diabetes.

  • Week 1

    Our very first week at AI camp we were introduced to what we would be doing. With little knowledge on the subject of Python we kicked-off strong!Learning all the basics and putting them into practice was the first hurdle of the race.

  • Week 2

    Week 1 had come and gone. Our next step was learning about models. This included SVMs, logistic regression, random forests, and more. Based on the data set we chose the first week we were to use the models we learned about to determine which model gave the best accuracy.

  • July 2020

    Week 3

    Our final step for week three was application. Based on all the information from the data we gathered and all the graphs we made that interpreted the information we had to create a website. Thus, bringing us to this exact website!
